## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### General

**Q: Where can I download your apps?**  
A: All my apps are available exclusively on the [Apple App Store](#).

**Q: Are your apps free to use?**  
A: Most apps are free to download with optional in-app purchases or subscriptions for premium features. Please check the specific App Store listing for details.

### Troubleshooting

**Q: The app is crashing or freezing. What should I do?**  
A: Please try the following steps:
1. Force close the app and reopen it.
2. Check the App Store for any available updates.
3. Restart your device.
4. If the issue persists, please delete and reinstall the app (Note: Ensure your data is backed up if the app does not use iCloud sync).

**Q: How do I restore my purchases?**  
A: Go to the app's **Settings** page and look for a "Restore Purchases" button. Tap it to restore any previously purchased premium features.

### Privacy & Data

**Q: Do you collect my personal data?**  
A: I prioritize user privacy. Most apps operate offline or store data in your personal iCloud. For detailed information, please review the Privacy Policy.
