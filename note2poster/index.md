# Privacy Policy for Note to Post

**Last Updated:** April 25, 2026

Your privacy is important to us. It is Note to Post's policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect from you across our application, Note to Post, and other sites we own and operate.

## 1. Information We Collect

### Personal Data
**We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.** Note to Post is designed to be an offline-first application.

### User Content
All content you create within Note to Post, including:
- Text imported from Apple Notes
- Images imported from Apple Notes
- Table content extracted from notes
- Poster layouts and theme configurations
- Custom signatures added during export

...is processed entirely on your device and is never uploaded to our servers.

## 2. Permissions and Access

To function correctly, Note to Post may request access to certain features on your device. You can manage these permissions at any time in your device's Settings.

### Photo Library
- **Reason:** Note to Post requests write-only access to your photo library to allow you to save exported poster images to your Camera Roll.
- **Privacy:** We only save images that you explicitly choose to export. We do not read, scan, or access any other photos in your library.

### Apple Notes (Share Extension)
- **Reason:** Note to Post integrates with iOS Share Extension to receive content you choose to share from Apple Notes.
- **Privacy:** Only the specific note content you actively share via the Share Sheet is transferred to the app. We do not access your entire Notes database.

## 3. Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
All your data is stored locally on your device. If you delete the Note to Post app, your data (including imported notes and poster configurations) will be permanently deleted from the device unless you have backed it up via iCloud Device Backup or iTunes.

### iCloud Backup
If you have iCloud Backup enabled on your device, your local app data may be backed up to Apple's iCloud servers securely, governed by Apple's privacy policy.

## 4. Purchases

Note to Post is a paid download app available on the App Store.
- **Transactions:** All financial transactions are handled directly by Apple via the App Store. We do not process, store, or have access to your credit card or banking information.

## 5. Third-Party Services

We do not use third-party analytics services (like Google Analytics or Mixpanel) or advertising networks that track you across apps.

## 6. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. As our app does not collect personal data, it is safe for users of all ages.

## 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
